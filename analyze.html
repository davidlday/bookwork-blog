Use this form to analyze a piece of writing with the same algorithms I use. Some important points:

  • Click once. Be patient. It takes a few seconds, and I haven't figured out how to add one of these neat spinner things to let you know it's processing.
  • Nothing from this is saved anywhere. Not the story; not the results. If you want to save your results, write them down or print the page.
  • Plain text only.
  • Everything between double quotes (") is considered dialogue.
  • Section separators (e.g. #) are ignored as long as there are no word characters used. Actually, this isn't important right now, but may be at some point.
  • Paragraphs are separated by a line break (return). Multiple line breaks are considered considered as one.
  • The formulae for the readability scores vary. What you get here may not exactly match what you get somewhere else, but it's close enough.
  • Don't send your novel through. This is for short stories only (say, less than 10,000 words).
